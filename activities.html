Hot Air Balloon Rides
in Cappadocia, Turkey

The best place to feel weightless in a basket and soar 3,000 feet above the ground is in Cappadocia, Turkey. Every morning, hundreds of balloons soar in the sky. These hot air balloon rides are offered about 250 days each year in Cappadocia, making exploration of the winter wonderland possible. The balloons travel at speeds ranging from 2 to 22 km/hr.

Water Sports at
Lake Eildon, Australia

Lake Eildon is located in Australia, and the lake is six times the size of Sydney Harbor. It's a paradise for people who enjoy watersports. At Lake Eildon, water skiing, wakeboarding, kayaking, boating, swimming, and fishing are all made possible. Whether it's getting tugged by a speedboat at 20 mph or swimming leisurely, the lake's gorgeous scenery is a sight to behold.

Rafting and Ziplining
over Vilcanota River, Peru

The Vilcanota River is located in Peru. It is where the Cusco Rafting and Zipline Adventure takes place, and the adventure lasts 8 hours. As people whitewater raft the Peruvian rapids and feel the rush going down the 2 thrilling ziplines, they get to enjoy the beautiful landscapes. Going to the Vilcanota River means an adrenaline-filled day is awaiting.
