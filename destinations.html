Infinity Pool, Singapore

This pool is located at Marina Bay Sands in Singapore. It is the world's longest and most elevated pool. The pool sits on three 55-story tall hotel towers, and it's only available to hotel guests. Also, although the pool's vanishing edge appears dangerous, it is safe due to a trough-like basin located just below the top edge that catches and returns water that flows over the edge.

Grand Prismatic Hot
Spring, Wyoming

This natural pool is located in Yellowstone National Park. It is the largest hot spring in the United States and the third-largest in the world. This spring is 370 feet in diameter, and the water comes from a crack in the Earth that is 121 feet deep. It gets its rainbow colors from different species of heating-loving bacteria surrounding the cooler areas of the spring.
