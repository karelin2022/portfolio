Mr. Ming’s Chinese
Dining, Hong Kong

Mr. Ming's signature dishes come from his grandfather's book of Chiu Chow and Cantonese recipes. It is his most prized posession, and the book has been passed down for generations. Some must-try dishes include Steamed Whole Crab with Pork Patty and Conpoy, Soyed French Foie Gras with Egg, and Smoked Duck with Bagasse in Chiu Chow Style. Along with their signature dishes, they also serve handcrafted Dim Sum made fresh daily. Specialties include Ming's Signature Steamed Shrimp Dumplings, Braised Pork Belly Buns with Preserved Vegetables, and Deep-Fried Abalone Pastries.

Attica in Melbourne, Australia

Attica is an elegant dining restaurant located in Ripponlea, Australia, one of Melbourne's suburbs. The owner and head chef of Attica is Ben Shewry. A meal at Attica can run to 20 or more courses and last 3 hours as servers explain the courses, where they came from, and how difficult they are to prepare. Some dishes include Shellfish BBQ, Pearl Oyster in Paperbark, Reko and Ben's Picnic Caviar, Killer Salad, and Beef Rib with Dorrigo Pepper.

Maido in Lima, Peru

Maido is a restaurant located in Lima, Peru. The owner and head chef of Maido is Mitsuharu Tsumura. He wanted to create a restaurant that served Nikkei cuisine (Peruvian-Japanese fusion cuisine). Some dishes include ceviche de lapas, sanguchito de paiche, and gyoza de cuy.
